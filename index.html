Cornered!

Instructions

There's been a zombie outbreak, and you can't run from the undead horde. You're backed against a wall, and your only chance for survival is to fight.

Choose an opponent to fight and then use the attack button to swing your machete. You've taken it down when its hit points (HP) reach 0.

Watch out, they'll be attacking you back! You have HP too, and if your hit points run out, you'll be joining their legion.
