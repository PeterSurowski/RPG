Cornered!

Instructions

You've been cornered by a horde of undead. Your only chance for survival is to fight.

Choose an opponent and use the attack button to swing your machete. You've defeated the monster when its hit points (HP) reach 0.

Watch out, they'll be attacking you back! If your HP runs out, you'll be joining their legion.
